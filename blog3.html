Atleti suffers but advances.

Exciting tie between atletico and inter. The colchoneros suffered but took the win on penalties.

Marcos Barzola

Yesterday Atleti and Inter gave us a tremendous match, two teams with a similar scheme and very intense led us to doubt which would be the result throughout the match. Inter came with an advantage for what they had done in the first leg (1-0), the Italian team was very solid as a home team, leaving us even a perception that they could have won the game by a wide margin.

On the other hand, Atleti not only came with the defeat at the hands of Inter, but also after losing several matches in the league, which made the fans doubt how the team would face this second leg. According to the data, Atleti is the strongest European team at home and again we saw why, they have a very passionate fan base that supplies them with energy from the moment the players get on the bus until the match ends.

To everyone's surprise, atleti showed its best version of itself this season, and came out to eat Inter. The Italian team got ahead on the scoreboard, but a few minutes later atleti discounted, and in the final minutes came Depay's goal that would take the team to extra time and then to extra time.
